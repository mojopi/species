Species Affected By Climate Change
A digital memorial.



After the global climate strike in 2019, a group of sixth graders, their art teachers, and a computer science teacher came together to make a digital memorial to species affected by climate change.

This is our memorial so far.

To make this memorial, we studied how to draw animals, how to make and mix colors, and, inspired by Matisse's collages, we made our own colors and painted papers with them. Those papers were put together in these collages to represent the species you'll see here.

To show what we're losing, in each depcition of a creature, there is also a depiction of its absence — just click and see.

You can hear our voices too.
